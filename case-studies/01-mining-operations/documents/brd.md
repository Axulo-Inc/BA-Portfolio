# Business Requirements Document
## Mining Operations Optimization Dashboard

### Project Overview
**Project:** Real-time Mining Operations Dashboard  
**Objective:** Reduce downtime by 12% through data-driven decision making  
**Stakeholders:** Operations Manager, Maintenance Supervisor, Safety Officer

### Problem Statement
Manual reporting systems caused 24-48 hour delays in decision-making, resulting in operational inefficiencies and increased downtime.

### Solution Requirements
1. **Real-time dashboard** with Power BI and Streamlit visualization
2. **Automated data pipeline** integrating IoT sensors and ERP systems
3. **Predictive analytics** for equipment maintenance
4. **Compliance tracking** with automated reporting

### Success Metrics
- 12% reduction in operational downtime
- 8% improvement in safety compliance
- Real-time decision making (from 24-48 hour delays)
- Estimated $2M annual savings

### Technical Implementation
- **Python ETL Pipeline** for data processing
- **SQLite/PostgreSQL** database for data storage
- **Power BI** for executive dashboards
- **Streamlit** for operational dashboards
