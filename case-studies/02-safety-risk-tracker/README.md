## 1. Executive Summary
Digital safety reporting system that improved reporting efficiency by 40% and reduced hazard resolution time from 48 hours to 1 hour.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
Manual paper-based safety reporting systems caused significant delays in hazard resolution (48+ hours), poor compliance tracking, and limited management visibility into safety performance.

---

## 3. Solution Overview
- Mobile-first application for instant hazard reporting
- Real-time management dashboard for compliance visibility
- Automated risk assessment using a 5x5 risk matrix
- Multi-channel notification system for immediate alerts
- Analytics-driven insights for trend identification and prevention

---

## 4. Results & Impact
- 40% improvement in reporting efficiency  
- Hazard resolution time reduced from 48 hours to 1 hour (98% improvement)  
- Real-time compliance visibility for management  
- Reporting compliance improved from 75% to 95%  
- Estimated annual cost savings through efficiency gains
